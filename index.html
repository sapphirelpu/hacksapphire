About HackSapphire

HackSapphire is going to be organized by student organization Sapphire , under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University, Phagwara, Punjab, India.
Our aim is to collaborate the intellects of programmers, designers, application
developers, tech-geeks and newbies in the world of programming. At HackSapphire, we want you to build something for our society, for the world. These ideas can generally be in the form of websites, applications, projects or posters. You’ll have all the freedom to create a product, learn new techniques for your future, or just have fun working on a
project with friends. Moreover, we’ll offer you a chance to network with
other creators & professionals.

More about HackSapphire

We will release some problem statements on the day of the hackathon after our inauguration ceremony ends, you will have to find a solution on the spot and then you will have to build it.
Its a 36 hrs hackathon where you will get 24hrs to build your project and rest of the time we will enjoy all together.
Oh yes, don't worry, our mentors will assist you when you need help.
